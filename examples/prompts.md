## Tips for Writing Prompts

1. **Be Specific**: Include style, colors, materials, and fixtures
2. **Add Lighting**: Mention the type of lighting (natural, warm, dramatic, etc.)
3. **Include Scale**: Describe room size if important
4. **Style Keywords**: minimalist, modern, vintage, farmhouse, industrial, bohemian, etc.
5. **Mood**: cozy, luxurious, functional, creative, serene, etc.
